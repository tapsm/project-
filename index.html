About Us

Discover the beauty of the Luxury Blue Mountain... Lilac Valley Farm resort is a simple, rustic farm stay accomodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains. The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown barn three years ago with a plan plan to renovate. After years of hard work, the property now features a beautifully restored, open plan,luxury barn house, on four acres of land home to sleep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named ROSIE. There is an outdoor plunge pool, just off the back erandah and a stunning cottage garden. The lilac Valley farm-Stay has been increadibly popular on Air BnB, and was even featured in a handful of online articles.
